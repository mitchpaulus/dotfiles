# Gutters

Supplies

- 5" K style gutters
- Gutter sleeves for connections
- Self tapping gutter screws
- 2 end caps
- Red/Green tin snips
- Gutter hangers
- Gutter silicone

Per [this guy](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HI-W8e7ICnw)

- Gutter hangers every 18" or 24". (20 for 30')
- 1/2" or 1/4" slope per 10 ft.
