Carrier Serial Year

Source/Credit: <https://www.building-center.org/carrier-hvac-age/>

Example serial number styles/formats found:
Style 1:     4006A17330
Style 2:     850304091
Style 3:     W4D14008 (U.S.) ~or~  4WD14008 (Canada)
Style 4:     A167890
Style 5:     6###### (U.S.) ~or~ 6#### (Canada)
Style 6:    46U152456 or 2W13270  (one or two digits followed by a letter) Bryant & Carrier

Style 1: 4 numbers, letter, 5 numbers (10 total)
- First two are week
- Next two are two digit year.

Style 2: 9 digits
  - First two are year
  - Next two are month

Style 4: A167890, A-L 0-9 5 digits (7 total)
