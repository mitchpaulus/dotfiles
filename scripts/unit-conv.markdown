# Unit Conversions

## Length

1 ft = 0.3048 m

## Area

1 m² = 10.7639 ft² = 1562500 / 145161 ft²

## Energy

3.6 x 10^6 J = 1 kWh

## Volume

1 m³ = 35.315 ft³

## Volumetric Flow

1 m³/s = 2118.88 ft³/min (CFM)

1 (m³/s) / m² = 196.9 CFM/ft²
1 CFM/ft² = 5.08e-3 (m³/s) / m²

1 m³/s = 15,850 gpm

## Thermal Conductivity

1 BTU/(ft²-hr-°F) = 5.68 W/(m²-K)

## Power

3412 BTU/h = 3.412 kBTU/h = 1 kW

1 hp = 0.746 kW
1 kW = 1.341 hp

3.517 kW = 1 ton
